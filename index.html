RESTAURANT

The restaurant is located next to Mr. Hung Hotel. This is a place to relax and enjoy home-cooked meals in a pleasant atmosphere.
You can choose to sit inside the big hall with ceiling-to-floor window or outside at the open-air veranda.
Our menu features delicious and familiar Italian mains, wine and fresh drinks.
Despite our name, we offer Vietnamese cuisine as well. Try some of traditional sets, hot pot or fresh spring rolls set. Our Vietnamese dishes have options for vegetarians.
After a good meal, you can continue to chat at the pool table on the rooftop while sipping beers or our bar cocktails.

About Meo Vac

Located at the end of the Happiness Road, Meo Vac Town has an area of 14.41 square kilometres and a population of over 5,000 people. This is a center of economic and cultural exchange of Meo Vac district. The system of accommodation establishments, catering services, and entertainment places is developing and basically meets the increasing needs of tourists. Visitors to Meo Vac Town will be able to experience many cultural spaces of the Stone Highlands.
